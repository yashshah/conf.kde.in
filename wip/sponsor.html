Sponsors

conf.kde.in 2011 was the first conference in India to focus primarily on Qt and KDE. Just like any other community event, conf.kde.in runs on volunteer support and sponsors. Sponsor support is very important because it helps us to cover the expenses for the event. If you are an organisation using Qt and KDE for your products and services, then this would be the ideal opportunity for you to showcase your Qt/KDE products and services to the world. Please download the following document to know more details regarding sponsorship http://kde.in/files/call-for-sponsors.pdf If you have any questions you can either contact us on conf@kde.in or via the contact form at http://kde.in/contact/ (recommended) .
